New Zealand Highlights

Discover the most breathtaking destinations and experiences New Zealand has to offer

Queenstown

Known as the adventure capital of the world, Queenstown offers bungee jumping, skydiving, and stunning alpine scenery.

Milford Sound

Famously described as the 'eighth wonder of the world', Milford Sound is a fjord of stunning natural beauty.

Hobbiton Movie Set

Visit the iconic film location of The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit trilogies in Matamata.

Waitomo Glowworm Caves

Experience the magical glow of thousands of tiny bioluminescent creatures in underground caves.

Auckland Sky Tower

Standing at 328 meters, the Sky Tower is Auckland's most recognizable landmark with panoramic city views.

Mount Cook

New Zealand's highest peak at 3,724 meters, offering spectacular alpine scenery and hiking opportunities.

Tasman Glacier

New Zealand's longest glacier, stretching 27 kilometers from the Southern Alps to Tasman Lake.

Rotorua Geothermal

Experience New Zealand's geothermal wonders, including bubbling mud pools, hot springs, and geysers.

South Island Adventure

Embark on an unforgettable journey through New Zealand's South Island, where you'll discover snow-capped mountains, turquoise lakes, ancient glaciers, and lush rainforests. This 7-day adventure showcases the best of the South Island's natural beauty and adventure activities.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the stunning landscapes of Fiordland National Park
  • Visit Milford Sound, described as the "eighth wonder of the world"
  • Experience a glacier hike on Franz Josef or Fox Glacier
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Lake Tekapo and the Southern Alps
  • Explore the adventure capital of Queenstown

Tour Details

  • Duration: 7 days / 6 nights
  • Departure: Christchurch
  • Group Size: Maximum 12 people
  • Accommodation: 4-star hotels and lodges
  • Meals: Breakfast daily, 3 dinners included

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Christchurch

Welcome to New Zealand! Arrive in Christchurch and transfer to your hotel. Evening welcome dinner.

Day 2: Christchurch to Queenstown

Scenic drive to Queenstown, stopping at Lake Tekapo and Mount Cook National Park.

Day 3: Queenstown Adventures

Choose from optional activities: bungee jumping, skydiving, or a scenic cruise on Lake Wakatipu.

Day 4: Queenstown to Milford Sound

Drive the scenic Milford Road to Milford Sound and enjoy a cruise through the fjord.

Day 5: Milford Sound to Franz Josef

Travel to the West Coast and explore the Franz Josef Glacier region.

Day 6: Glacier Experience

Guided glacier hike on Franz Josef Glacier, followed by a scenic flight over the Southern Alps.

Day 7: Franz Josef to Christchurch

Return to Christchurch via the scenic Lewis Pass. Tour concludes with farewell dinner.

North Island Discovery

Discover the cultural heart of New Zealand on the North Island. This 5-day journey takes you from the vibrant city of Auckland to the geothermal wonders of Rotorua, and the natural beauty of Waitomo Caves and Lake Taupo. Experience Maori culture, explore volcanic landscapes, and relax in natural hot springs.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore Auckland, New Zealand's largest city
  • Experience traditional Maori culture and performance
  • Visit the magical Waitomo Glowworm Caves
  • Relax in natural hot springs in Rotorua
  • Marvel at the powerful Huka Falls and Lake Taupo

Tour Details

  • Duration: 5 days / 4 nights
  • Departure: Auckland
  • Group Size: Maximum 10 people
  • Accommodation: 3-4 star hotels and lodges
  • Meals: Breakfast daily, 2 dinners included

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Auckland

Welcome to New Zealand! Arrive in Auckland and transfer to your hotel. Evening at leisure.

Day 2: Auckland to Rotorua

Travel to Rotorua, stopping at Waitomo Caves to experience the glowworm caves. Evening Maori cultural performance and dinner.

Day 3: Rotorua Exploration

Explore Rotorua's geothermal wonders, including Wai-O-Tapu Thermal Wonderland and the Pohutu Geyser. Relax in natural hot springs.

Day 4: Rotorua to Lake Taupo

Travel to Lake Taupo, New Zealand's largest lake. Visit the powerful Huka Falls and enjoy optional activities such as skydiving or fishing.

Day 5: Lake Taupo to Auckland

Return to Auckland, stopping at Hobbiton Movie Set (optional). Tour concludes with farewell dinner.

Complete Island Experience

From the cultural attractions of the North Island to the stunning natural landscapes of the South Island, this tour offers a complete New Zealand experience. Itinerary

North Island (Days 1-5)

Explore Auckland's top attractions, including the Sky Tower and Waiheke Island.
